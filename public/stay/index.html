Travelling within Bangalore

The easiest way to travel around Bangalore would be to use ride sharing apps such as Lyft, Uber and Ola, or to hail auto-rickshaws. Additionally, the Metro runs between some key parts of the city.

Bangalore is a very large city and there is a lot you could do, but the traffic can be very unpredictably brutal, so give yourself wide buffers when planning travel within the city.
